A little about Luis

I’m a software developer with extensive experience in React, Redux, Rails, Javascript, and Ruby. I went into software development because I loved the field’s combination of problem solving and design.

After I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with a degree in Technical Writing, I worked at an online publisher for 2 years as an editor,.

I managed a staff of 26 people and answered to millions of readers. But I found that I spent more and more of my time fixing problems with the site than I did actually editing or writing articles, and that I really enjoyed doing it. That exposure motivated me to make a career change to software development.

That’s why I went to App Academy. The program was extremely challenging and fast paced, but ultimately rewarding. I got to build great projects there like a YouTube clone and a web animations-teaching site.
